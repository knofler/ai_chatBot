What is this chatbot?
This is an AI-powered chatbot designed to assist users with questions and provide helpful information.

How can I customize the chatbot?
You can customize the chatbot by modifying the training data, adjusting the conversation flow, and integrating with your preferred platforms and APIs.

What are the different tiers available?
There are three tiers: Basic (website integration), Standard (multi-platform & API), and Advanced (full business integration).

How do I integrate the chatbot with my website?
Simply add the provided JavaScript widget code to your website, and ensure the backend server is running.

Is the conversation secure?
Yes, all conversations are processed securely using industry-standard encryption and best practices.
